Khaptad Experience

Three years ago, in April, we set off on an adventure to Khaptad, a hidden gem nestled in the Himalayas of Nepal. The lush green landscapes and serene environment promised an escape from the hustle and bustle of city life. As we made our way there, we were filled with excitement and anticipation. The winding roads and breathtaking views along the journey were just a hint of the beauty that awaited us.

In Khaptad, we spent a full day hiking through pristine forests, surrounded by the vibrant sounds of nature. Each step led us deeper into a world where time seemed to stand still. The majestic views from the hilltops were nothing short of magical, and we often found ourselves pausing just to soak it all in. We also had the chance to connect with the locals, who welcomed us with open arms. Their stories and traditions added another layer of richness to our experience.

Reflecting on our trip, we realized how important it is to step away from our daily routines and embrace nature. Khaptad taught us the value of tranquility and the beauty of simple moments. If you ever plan to visit Khaptad, we recommend going in spring when the flowers bloom, and the weather is perfect for trekking. Don’t forget to pack your camera, as every corner offers a postcard-worthy view! In conclusion, our trip to Khaptad was a journey of discovery, and we wholeheartedly recommend it to anyone seeking adventure and peace in nature.

Khaptad Journey Itinerary

  • Day 1: Kathmandu to Dhangadhi:
    We started our adventure with a scenic flight from Kathmandu to Dhangadhi. The vibrant landscapes unfolded below as we soared through the skies. Upon landing, we hopped on a local bus to Dipayal, where we were greeted by the warm hospitality of the locals.

  • Day 2: Jhingrana to Khaptad
    Early the next morning, we set off on a hike from Jhingrana to Khaptad. The trek was invigorating, with breathtaking views of the surrounding hills and forests. As we approached Khaptad National Park, we felt a sense of tranquility wash over us.

  • Days 3 & 4: Exploring Khaptad
    These two days were filled with exploration. We visited the serene Khaptad Lake, where the peaceful waters reflected the sky. The Khaptad Baba Ashram offered a spiritual retreat, and we found solace in its quietude. Ghoda Daha and Triveni Patan were highlights, each steeped in natural beauty and cultural significance. We truly felt connected to nature during this time.

  • Day 5: Khaptad to Achham
    On our fifth day, we hiked to the Sahasralinga Temple, famous for its sacred stone carvings. The spiritual ambiance was palpable, and we took a moment to reflect on the beauty surrounding us before making our way to Achham.

  • Day 6: Achham to Dhangadhi
    We traveled back to Dhangadhi by bus, reminiscing about the incredible experiences we had in Khaptad. The journey allowed us to soak in more of the vibrant local culture along the way.

  • Day 7: Dhangadhi to Kathmandu
    We concluded our trip with a return flight to Kathmandu, carrying with us the unforgettable memories and the peaceful spirit of Khaptad. This journey was a perfect blend of nature, culture, and spirituality, leaving a lasting impression on our hearts.

    • Reflecting on our adventure, we encourage anyone looking for a unique travel experience to explore the enchanting Khaptad region!
